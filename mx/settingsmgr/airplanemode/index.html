Controls whether the device user can access "Airplane Mode" from the Android Settings and Quick Settings panels, and from the Power-key menu on devices running Android 5.x Marshmallow and older. When a device enters Airplane Mode, its Bluetooth, cellular and Wi-Fi radios are disabled; Bluetooth and Wi-Fi radios can be turned back on if desired by the user. See Power Key Manager for controlling Airplane mode from the Power Off menu. Airplane Mode is discontinued on devices running Android 8.0 Oreo and later.
